CHESS-ID-7A BioSAXS Beamline

Biological Small Angle X-ray Scattering at CHESS

About the Beamline

CHESS-ID-7A is dedicated to Biological Small Angle X-ray Scattering (BioSAXS), supporting studies of macromolecular size, shape, and conformational changes in solution. The beamline is equipped for high-throughput, automated experiments and is co-sponsored by MacCHESS and CHEXS.

Beamline Specifications

  • Energy range: 8–13 keV
  • q-range: ~0.01 – 0.35 Å⁻¹
  • Detector: Pilatus 100K/300K (or update to current)
  • Automated sample changer and temperature control
  • Beam size: ~250 µm × 250 µm

Sample Requirements

Typical sample concentrations range from 1–10 mg/mL depending on the scattering power of the molecule. Buffer-matched controls are essential. Please consult the user guide for detailed preparation protocols.
